Cookie Export Guide

Chrome Installation

  1. Download the Cookie Scout extension from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" and confirm the installation.
  3. Pin the extension for easy access.
  4. Click the icon and connect to OmniBox.

Firefox Installation

  1. Download the Cookie Scout extension from Firefox Add-ons.
  2. Click "Add to Firefox" and grant permissions.
  3. Pin the extension to your toolbar.
  4. Click the icon and connect to OmniBox.
